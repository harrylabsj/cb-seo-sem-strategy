## Description: <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
SEO specialists, performance marketers, growth leads, and international ecommerce teams use this skill to plan market-specific SEO and SEM strategy before launching or expanding in a country or region. It helps map local search ecosystems, localize intent, choose site architecture, allocate paid-search budget, and define measurement checkpoints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Search-engine algorithms, advertising policies, privacy requirements, and local marketing regulations change frequently by market. <br>
Mitigation: Verify current local SEO, SEM, privacy, and ad-platform rules before launching campaigns. <br>
Risk: Market-share, platform, policy, and regulatory examples may become outdated or may not match a specific product category. <br>
Mitigation: Treat generated strategy as a planning starting point and confirm assumptions with current market data or local search marketing professionals. <br>
